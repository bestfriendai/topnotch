We're here to help you get the most out of Top Notch.

Getting Started

Top Notch runs as a menu bar app. After launching, hover over the notch area at the top of your screen to see the collapsed view, then click to expand the full dashboard.

System Requirements

  • macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later
  • Works best on MacBook Pro models with a notch (2021 or later)
  • Also works on MacBooks and external displays without a notch

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control music?

Top Notch detects when Apple Music or Spotify is playing and shows controls automatically. Hover over the notch to see the current track, or click to expand for full playback controls.

How does the weather widget work?

Enter your city name in the weather widget settings. Top Notch uses the Open-Meteo API to fetch weather data. No location permission is required.

How do I play YouTube videos?

Copy a YouTube URL to your clipboard. Top Notch will detect it and offer to play the video directly in the notch. You can also paste URLs manually in the YouTube widget.

Can I customize which widgets appear?

Yes. Open Settings from the menu bar icon or right-click the notch. You can enable or disable each widget individually.

Is my data private?

Yes. Top Notch runs entirely on your Mac. No accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection. See our Privacy Policy for details.
